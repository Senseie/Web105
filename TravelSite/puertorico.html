San Juan

The Capitol City of San Juan, Puerto Rico has as much charm as she holds the mysteries of the Pirates of the Carribean. The block buster movie series was set somewhere amongst the beautiful islands surrounding the main isle of Puerto Rico

The Capitol holds the old fort where there were many battles held protecting this beautiful island of mystery. The natives call themselves Taino! Native Puerto Rican Indian cultures ruled these lands for many centuries until the white man came and conquered.

Beaches

The incandescent and irresistable beaches of the Enchanted island of Puerto Rico has it's draw. A favorite among tourists and surfers alike. From the professional surf beaches of Rincon to the humble family beaches of Camuy this island seems to have it all.

All the beaches are public domain! Which means anyone can walk on the beaches at any time without trespassing. With the exception of the Grandfather beaches of a handful of resorts where they in fact own there part of the beach.
